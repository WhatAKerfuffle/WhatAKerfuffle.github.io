What A Kerfuffle is a funky, weird, and all around bizarre place where I throw all of my random artwork onto. The name stems from my typical online alias - Kerfuffle. It's my favorite word - yes those are a thing.

Kerfuffle - noun: a commotion or fuss, especially one caused by conflicting views.


My real name is Jessica Schlesiger. I am a student at Shippensburg University studying computer science located in Shippensburg, Pennsylvania. I have been actively drawing since highschool and have been coding since middle school. The purpose of What A Keruffle was to shake off the dust of my HTML/CSS skills and provide a location to proudly my display artwork.
